# Bricks — Go to market (founding pilot)

**Audience:** Belle Property + Ray White contacts, Joe (engineer), Matt (operator).

## The offer (60-day founding partner)

**Free. Co-branded. Max 2 agents.**

| They get | They give |
|----------|-----------|
| Private listing preview (web) + share cards from detail | 5–10 properties fed like live listings |
| Achievement card per property (screenshot / share) | Use preview in 3 real pitches |
| Badges on catalogue + sort = ranking | Post 2 social cards |
| 30-year cashflow brick on every property | 30-min debrief at day 60 |

**Pitch line:** *"Feed your stock privately, rank it, show the vendor the 30-year brick on your phone — not live on Bricks yet."*

## Pricing discovery (ask in debrief)

- Per vendor report (like photos)
- Per listing pack (report + card + rank)
- Monthly tool fee (only after pilot proves value)
- Broker referral per funded loan (line two, not the agent pitch)

## Build sequence (fast path)

1. Research + 30-year brick (hero) — sort filters = ranking
2. Detail achievements + **share card** (social)
3. Agent **listing preview** (private web page, footer — not PDF, not top nav)
4. REAXML + billing — **after** paid yes

No per-suburb leaderboard pages until enough agent-fed data.
